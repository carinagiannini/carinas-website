Carina's background

Early Life

Carina Giannini grew up in Rochester, NY, and lived there her whole life unil she went to college. She went to elementary school at a public school only two doors down from her home, where she met her childhood friends that she has stayed close with to this day.
School of the Arts

High school

Carina went to high school at School of the Arts, a specialized high school focusing on the arts like drama, dance, and theater tech. Carina was a Creative Writing major there, and joined Varsity track when she was only in 7th grade. Her true passion, however, she found in 8th grade at a Magical Journey thru STAGES, a local community theater. Carina, thinking she might like to switch her major at school to theater tech, decided to try teching a show or two at STAGES, and soon fell in love with it. While she never switched her major, she continued working at STAGES until she left for college. Through the years, she worked every show and learned all she could, and eventually rose up the ranks to become the head Sound Engineer, training other kids in sound design and microphone handing.
